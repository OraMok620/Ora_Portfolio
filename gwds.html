Project Overview

For this project, I designed a complete brand identity and built a responsive website for a fictional non-profit "Pay As You Feel" restaurant that rescues surplus food from supermarkets to provide meals for the community. I created a restaurant name and logo, produced three mockups showing branding in real-world and developed a mobile-first website using Bootstrap 5 featuring a rotating menu, reservation form, location details, and food box collection information. The goal was to create a cohesive brand experience that helps the restaurant communicate its mission of reducing food waste while serving everyone with dignity, regardless of their ability to pay.
