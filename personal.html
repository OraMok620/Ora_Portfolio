Other experience

From secondary school to my third year at university, I organised group orders for anime merchandise for around 6 years. What started as a personal hobby gradually developed into a small-scale operation involving pricing strategy, spreadsheet-based financial tracking, logistics coordination, and social media customer management. After moving to the UK, I continued managing the project remotely while delegating local fulfilment to a collaborator.

My role

  1. Tracking for new merchandise information.
  2. Tracked trend on popularity trend of game characters
  3. Developed pricing adjustments based on character popularity and market demand.
  4. Edit the image, make sure the information (text) in image is clearly visible.
  5. Monitored exchange rates (JPY to HKD) to calculate item pricing.
  6. Set selling prices based on cost structure. For example, if the exchange rate was 0.05, the price would be adjusted to around 0.06 to include shipping cost.
  7. Maintained structured payment records and order tracking.
  8. Keeping communication

Workflow

  1. Tracking for new merchandise information.
  2. Post into social media (looking for anyone want other characters’ merchandise in the same box)
  3. Price adjustments based on character popularity.
  4. Open group chat for managing announcements (such as arrival of merchandise, how to get the merchandise after its arrival)
  5. Collecting payment
  6. Keep tracking for when the merchandise is sent from online shop and when it will arrive to the container warehouse
  7. After the arrival of merchandise, communicate with collaborator, asking for help for handing over merchandise to customers or sending small parcel to customers.
